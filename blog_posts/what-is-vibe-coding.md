# What is Vibe Coding?

Vibe coding is the practice of building software by **describing what you want** to an AI assistant — Claude, Cursor, Lovable, Bolt, v0 — and letting it generate the code, instead of typing every line yourself.

## Why people love it
- Ship a working prototype in a weekend instead of a quarter
- Skip boilerplate and focus on the idea
- Learn by reading what the AI produces

## Why it's not magic
You still need taste, judgment, and the patience to debug. The AI gets you to *a* solution; making it *the* solution is on you.
