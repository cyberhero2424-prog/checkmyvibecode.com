Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026  ·  checkmyvibecode.com

1. Who We Are

CheckMyVibeCode ("we", "us", "our") is a community platform for showcasing AI-generated software projects, accessible at checkmyvibecode.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: support@checkmyvibecode.com

2. What Data We Collect

Account data — When you sign up or log in via Google or GitHub OAuth, we receive your email address, name, and profile picture from the respective provider. We store this data to identify your account.

User-generated content — Projects you submit, comments you post, and upvotes you cast are stored in our database and visible to other users.

Usage data — If you have consented to analytics cookies, we collect anonymized usage data via Google Analytics 4 (page views, session duration, general location at country level). No personally identifiable information is sent to Google Analytics.

Technical data — Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, referrer) may be recorded automatically. These are used for security and debugging purposes only.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the platform (authentication, displaying your projects and profile)
  • To allow other users to interact with your content (upvotes, comments)
  • To analyze how the platform is used and improve it (analytics, only with consent)
  • To protect the platform from abuse and ensure security

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling.

4. Third-Party Services

Supabase — Our backend infrastructure and database is hosted by Supabase, Inc. Your account data and content are stored in Supabase's EU-region servers. Supabase acts as a data processor under our instructions. Supabase Privacy Policy

Google Analytics 4 — If you consent, we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google may transfer data to servers outside the EU. You can opt out at any time via the cookie banner or by using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. Google Privacy Policy

Google & GitHub OAuth — When you log in via Google or GitHub, their respective privacy policies apply to the authentication process. We only receive and store the data they share with us (email, name, avatar).

5. Cookies

We use the following cookies and local storage:

  • Authentication session — Stored in localStorage via Supabase to keep you logged in. Essential for the platform to work.
  • Theme preference — We save your dark/light mode choice in localStorage.
  • Cookie consent — We store your analytics cookie preference in localStorage.
  • Google Analytics cookies — Only set if you accept analytics cookies. Used to measure traffic and usage.

EU visitors see a cookie banner on their first visit and can freely accept or decline analytics cookies.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU/EEA, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to processing of your data for analytics purposes
  • Withdraw consent — Withdraw analytics consent at any time by declining cookies in the banner

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@checkmyvibecode.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data and content for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the date of the last update at the top of this page. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at support@checkmyvibecode.com
